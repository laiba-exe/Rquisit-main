FEATURED BRAND

- Jacto

Jacto is a line of innovative demi-fine jewelry hand-made in NYC. It was founded in 2015 by Melanie Maggio, a footwear designer who left behind a lucrative career to create a sustainable, zero-waste accessory brand using the skin, bones, and teeth left over from commercial fishing. The raw material is sourced from sustainable fisheries and then transported to a taxidermist upstate, where flesh-eating beetles clean it naturally. The bones are cast on 45th Street in the Jewelry District, and all the chains and findings are sourced in Manhattan.

"SeaBones"

Physical + Virtual Collection

SeaBones is Jacto's first Phyigital (Physical and Virtual) collection. Like technology, the ocean holds mysteries still unknown to humankind; it's also home to some of the most exquisite jewels in the world. Here, at the intersection of the sea and future tech, Jacto merges sea bones with precious metal and Augmented Reality to create a rare and exquisite collection that is the first of its kind.
